8.2 Distribution

Like all GNU utilities, the latest version of Wget can be found at the master GNU archive site ftp.gnu.org, and its mirrors. For example, Wget 1.19.4 can be found at https://ftp.gnu.org/pub/gnu/wget/wget-1.19.4.tar.gz
